Clock Out

Stay on top of your time and money with Clock Out, the simple yet powerful time and earnings tracker for anyone who works by the hour.

 Download on the App Store

Key Features:

  • One-Tap Clock In/Out: Start and stop your timer right from the Home screen.
  • Multiple Workplaces: Add as many jobs as you need — each with its own hourly wage, location lookup, notes, and custom settings.
  • Instant Summaries: See This Week vs Last Week earnings and total hours at a glance.
  • Monthly Breakdown: Dive into detailed monthly reports — total hours, gross earnings, and comparisons to last month.
  • Shift History: Browse every shift you’ve worked, complete with date, duration, and dollars earned.
  • CSV Export: Easily back up or share your data for payroll, taxes, or personal records.
  • Sample Data & Debug Tools: Quickly populate sample shifts for testing or onboarding.
  • Privacy-First: All your data stays on your device. No accounts, no tracking, no surprises.

Whether you’re juggling side gigs, hourly work, or a full-time position, Clock Out makes it dead simple to monitor your progress and maximize your earnings. Download now and clock in on your financial goals!
